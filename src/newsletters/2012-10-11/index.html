Here is your link to the Tampa Bay area newsletter: http://dunedin.florida.usa.taoist.org/pdf/201210issue39.pdf

This newsletter is a little late coming to you while we got our new computer up and running.

This weekend will be the Southeast Region Annual General Meeting and Tai Chi Workshop hosted by St. Pete. On Friday, they will have a reception dinner at 6pm followed by an open forum from 7 to 8pm.

On Saturday, October 20, we will be having the monthly Instructor Class from 11am to 2pm. A $5 donation for lunch would be appreciated.

Our annual Daytime Intensive will be on Friday, November 2 from 9am to noon followed by lunch. The intensive is $25 and $35 pays for the intensive and lunch.

Don't forget, Sunday, November 4 is when Daylight Saving Time ends (set clocks back one hour).

November 7 to 11 is the International Tai Chi workshop in Tallahassee. Sat. & Sun. (10 & 11) is also CIT Weekend. Cost for 7 to 9 is $175 (plus meals) and 10 & 11 is $100 (plus meals). Please fax or email your registration forms as soon as possible, especially if you are requesting billeting.

The Miami branch will be hosting a Lok Hup (first half) workshop December 1 & 2. Word is Joe Thibeault from Montreal will be leading. Also, they will be hosting a Bahama cruise Dec. 3 to 7. Rumor has it that 20 people have signed up for the cruise and more than half are from Brandon. Way to represent!

The Brandon Holiday bash will be Sunday, December 9 at 4pm.
