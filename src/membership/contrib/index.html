Membership Contribution Schedule

The Society depends on the contributions of its members. Though the contributions are voluntary donations, it is our hope that you will support the Society at the level suggested so that we can continue to make the benefits of the [% abbr.ttcs %]® internal arts and methods available and to help others.

Registration · $20 non-refundable Registration fee applicable to adults and children. Payable when a person registers for the first time.

Membership Contributions

Adult $45 monthly or $115 quarterly or $440 annually
Full Time Student $35 monthly or $85 quarterly or $320 annually
Family $65 monthly or $175 quarterly or $680 annually
Senior Citizens $30 monthly or $70 quarterly or $260 annually (ages 60 & over)
Youths $20 monthly or $80 annually
Children $15 monthly or $45 annually
Minors are exempt if at leaszt one parent is a member in good standing

New Member Package · Students taking Beginner Classes for the first time may take advantage of this package, which combines the 4 month beginning class, one month of continuing class, and the registration fee, offering a savings of about one month's contributions.

Adult $165
Full Time Student $135
Seniors $100
Family $245 -- Includes two $20 registrations. If more than two members are included in a family, each additional family member contributes a $20 registration fee.

Contribution Schedule · We prefer that members make their contributions at the beginning of each month. New Member Package contributions are given at the start of the beginner's class. Quarterly and Annual contributions are made according to member's registration date and may also be made on a calendar basis.

Exemptions · Members unable to make contributions becuase of unemployment or extreme financial hardship may apply to the Branch council for a temporary exemption or reduction in order to remain a member in good standing. This exemption or reduction shall be reviewed by the Branch Council every three months.
