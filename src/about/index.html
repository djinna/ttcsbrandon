The International Taoist Tai Chi Society® is an international volunteer, non-profit organization with branches in over half of the states within the United States and more than 25 countries around the world.

Our founder, Master Moy Lin-shin made it his life's work to teach the arts of Tai Chi (taijiquan), Lok Hup Ba Fa (liuhebafa), Taoist meditation and other traditional internal arts to all who wished to learn them.
