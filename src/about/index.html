The International Taoist Tai Chi Society is an international volunteer, non-profit organization with branches in over half of the states within the United States and more than 25 countries around the world.

Our founder, Master Moy Lin-shin made it his life's work to teach the arts of [% w("Tai_chi_chuan", "Tai Chi") %] (taijiquan), [% w("Liuhebafa", "Lok Hup Ba Fa") %] (liuhebafa), Taoist meditation and other traditional internal arts to all who wished to learn them.
