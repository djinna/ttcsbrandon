January

  • Report members in good standing total to RMC
  • Determine number of branch delegates 1
  • Schedule branch annual meeting 2
  • Process instructor accreditation update forms
  • Mail letters for last year's single donations greater than $250 by January 31

By Early Spring

  • Nominations committee determines ballot (BC seats and delegates) — suggested slate plus nominations from members (see bylaws)

Spring — Summer

  • Annual Branch meeting and election for eligible BC seats, regional and national delegates. Members appoint 2 members of next year's nominating committee.
  • Branch Council appoints President (from BC members), Secretary, Treasurer, 1 member of next year's nominating committee
  • Send election and appointments results to RMC, on provided form
  • Mid-year review of branch activities with RMC

Fall

  • Branch annual report, due prior to Annual Regional Meeting
  • Submit any workshop requests for next year
  • Verify list of branch class locations for insurance purposes (late Oct — early Nov)
  • Prepare and submit next year's activity plan to RMC by October 1
  • Prepare and submit next year's financial plan to RMC by November 15

December

  • Submit check requests for current year by December 15th
  • Make deposits before December 31st
  • Inventory property, submit by early January

1 Branches under 150 — 1 National, 3 Regional and alternates. See Bylaws for branches 150 and over. ↩

2 Annual branch meetings/elections must be held at least 15 days before the annual regional meeting. ↩

Nominations close 30 days before the annual branch meeting.
