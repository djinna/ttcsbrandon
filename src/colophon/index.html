Colophon

Like almost all aspects of the Taoist Tai Chi Society, this website has been maintained by a series of volunteers, most recently by Darren Chamberlain. Please direct all complaints about the design, content, and usability directly to him. However, all other inquiries should go through official channels.

Technical Mumbo-Jumbo

The site is hand-coded in (relatively) modern HTML, complete with metadata, and uses the fantastic 960 Grid System for layout, since I'm a very poor designer. The templates and content are stored in GitHub, edited in vim, and built using the Template Toolkit.

Some effort has been taken to ensure that pages validate correctly (using the W3's Validator), and are [% w("HTML5") %].

The calendar, location, and search pages are made possible by Google Calendar, Google Maps, and Google Custom Search Engines, respectively.
